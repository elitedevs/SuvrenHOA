## Tier 1 — Build Next (high impact, achievable solo)

### AI Meeting Summarizer
Record meeting on any device → auto-transcribe → extract action items → publish minutes to Arweave. Meeting minutes are the #1 thing boards fail to do. Automate entirely.
- **Tech:** Groq Whisper (already in stack) + Claude summarization + Arweave upload
- **Revenue impact:** Killer feature for Professional/Enterprise tiers
- **Effort:** 2-3 weeks

### Predictive Special Assessments
AI analyzes reserve fund trajectory, building age, maintenance history, weather data → predicts when special assessment needed and how much → gives boards years of warning.
- **Tech:** Historical reserve data + linear regression + weather API
- **Revenue impact:** Insurance companies would pay for this data
- **Effort:** 3-4 weeks

### HOA Treasury Yield (connects both products)
Communities park reserve funds in DeFi yield via TreasuryYield contract. Show returns on Suvren.co. HOA boards see reserves growing. Bridge between both products.
- **Tech:** TreasuryYield.sol already exists, needs frontend + yield source
- **Revenue impact:** Platform can take small management fee
- **Effort:** 2-3 weeks (contract exists)

## Tier 2 — Differentiators (6-12 month horizon)

### Zero-Knowledge Proof Residency
Prove you live in the community without revealing identity. ZK proofs for anonymous-but-valid voting. Bleeding-edge privacy applied to real governance.
- **Tech:** ZK-SNARKs (circom/snarkjs) or Semaphore protocol
- **Revenue impact:** Enterprise feature, regulatory advantage

### On-Chain Board Reputation
Individual reputation scores for board members — attendance, responsiveness, initiative. Public, tamper-proof track record that follows them. Accountability as a feature.
- **Tech:** Event tracking from existing contracts + scoring algorithm
- **Revenue impact:** Attracts quality board candidates

### Dispute Resolution Protocol
On-chain arbitration for neighbor disputes. Both parties stake, present evidence, community votes. Cheaper than lawyers, permanently documented.
- **Tech:** New smart contract + escrow + voting mechanism
- **Revenue impact:** Premium feature, reduces HOA legal costs

### Smart Insurance Integration
Community health score feeds insurance underwriting. Higher score = lower premiums. Insurance companies pay for data.
- **Tech:** API for insurance carriers + health score feed
- **Revenue impact:** B2B revenue stream beyond SaaS

## Tier 3 — Moonshots (12-24 month horizon)

### DAO-to-DAO Diplomacy
Adjacent HOA communities negotiate shared services through cross-community governance. Two DAOs voting on the same proposal. First-of-its-kind.
- **Revenue impact:** Network effects — every new community makes existing ones more valuable

### Community Token Launchpad
HOA launches local commerce token — pool snack bar, farmer's market, babysitting co-op. Managed via SuvrenHOA, tradeable on Suvren.co.
- **Revenue impact:** Transaction fees, platform stickiness

### Real Estate + Crypto Dashboard Merger
Property NFTs have real estate value. Suvren.co tracks crypto. Merge: total net worth across crypto + real estate, all on-chain. Nobody has this.
- **Revenue impact:** Premium Suvren.co feature, attracts real estate investors
