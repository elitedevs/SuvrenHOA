## 📋 FILING 3: PROVISIONAL PATENT

### Where to File
**USPTO EFS-Web** (electronic filing): https://patentcenter.uspto.gov/

### Filing Fee
- **$320** (micro entity) — you likely qualify as micro entity
- $640 (small entity) if micro doesn't apply

### Micro Entity Qualification
You qualify if ALL are true:
- [ ] Qualify as a small entity (fewer than 500 employees)
- [ ] Have not been named as inventor on more than 4 previously filed patent applications
- [ ] Did not have gross income exceeding 3× the median household income (~$225,000) in the prior year
- [ ] Have not assigned/licensed/conveyed rights to an entity that exceeds the gross income limit
