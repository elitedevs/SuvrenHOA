## TRUST & SECURITY SECTION

> **Section header:** Built to last. Built to trust.

---

### 🔐 Built on Proven Technology
SuvrenHOA runs on the same distributed record-keeping technology used by major financial institutions worldwide. Every transaction and vote is recorded on an independent public ledger — meaning no single person, company, or server controls the data. It's the most tamper-resistant record system ever built.

*In plain English: think of it like a filing cabinet that thousands of independent computers all hold a copy of — nobody can slip in and change a file without everyone noticing.*

---

### 🗄️ Your Data, Permanently Safe
Your documents are stored on Arweave, a permanent storage network designed to preserve data for 200+ years. Unlike traditional cloud storage that can be deleted, corrupted, or shut down — your community's records on SuvrenHOA are designed to outlast any company, server, or hard drive.

Even if your HOA stops using SuvrenHOA tomorrow, your records will still be accessible. Forever.

---

### 👁️ Open and Auditable
The rules that govern your community's treasury and voting are open for anyone to inspect — including independent security researchers. We're not asking you to take our word for it. The code is public, the records are public, and the math doesn't lie.

---

### 🚫 We Never Touch Your Money
SuvrenHOA can't move your community's funds — period. Treasury transactions are governed by automated rules that only execute when the right approvals are in place. Your money is controlled by your community's governance rules, not by us.

## FAQ SECTION

> **Section header:** Questions? We've got answers.

---

**Do I need cryptocurrency to use SuvrenHOA?**

No. You don't need to buy, own, or understand any cryptocurrency to use SuvrenHOA. Sign up with your email and start using it like any other web application. We handle all the technical infrastructure behind the scenes — you just run your HOA.

---

**Is this secure?**

More secure than any traditional HOA software. Traditional software stores your data on servers that can be hacked, corrupted, or shut down. SuvrenHOA stores your community's records across thousands of independent computers worldwide — there's no central point of failure, and records can't be altered after the fact.

---

**What if I'm not tech-savvy?**

If you can use email and a web browser, you can use SuvrenHOA. We designed every screen for people who have zero interest in technology — they just want their HOA to work. Our average board member is in their 60s. We built this for them.

---

**Can the board manipulate votes?**

No. Once a vote is cast, it's permanently recorded and cannot be changed — not by the board, not by us, not by anyone. Every election comes with a complete audit trail that any resident can review at any time. The results are what they are, and they always will be.

---

**What happens if SuvrenHOA goes out of business?**

Your governance records are stored on a permanent, decentralized network that no single company controls — including us. Even if SuvrenHOA ceased to exist tomorrow, every vote, every document, and every financial record your community created would remain permanently accessible. You own your data.

---

**How much does it cost?**

Plans start at $49/month for communities up to 50 units. All plans include a 60-day free trial — no credit card required. See our [Pricing](#pricing-section) section above, or [contact us] if you have a larger community with special needs.

---

**Can I migrate from our current HOA software?**

Yes. We'll help you migrate your existing documents, resident lists, and financial records. Our onboarding team walks you through the process, and we provide templates to make data import as painless as possible. Most communities are fully set up within a week.

---

**Is there a long-term contract?**

No contracts, no lock-in, cancel anytime. We believe if SuvrenHOA is genuinely making your community's governance better, you'll stay — we don't need to trap you. Month-to-month billing, and you can export your data anytime.

---

**Can residents access the records too, or just the board?**

Both. Board members manage the HOA — they run elections, approve expenses, and upload documents. Residents have read access to the voting results, treasury dashboard, and document archive. Transparency isn't just for the board; it's for the whole community.

---

**What does "permanently recorded" actually mean?**

It means no one can change it. Not the board. Not the property manager. Not us. When a vote is cast or a document is uploaded, it's written to an independent public record that anyone can verify. It's mathematically impossible to alter — the same way you can't change a math equation after it's already been solved.
