# SuvrenHOA — Explainer Video Script
> **Duration:** ~90 seconds  
> **Format:** Hybrid — animated problem sequence → real product demo → CTA  
> **Tone:** Trustworthy, warm, slightly urgent. Like a financial advisor who genuinely wants to help — not a tech bro pitching Web3.  
> **Audience:** HOA board members, prospective adopters

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **VO Artist direction:** Warm, measured, authoritative. Think: a trusted neighbor who works in finance. Conversational but confident. Not salesy.
- **Music:** Soft, modern — something between a documentary score and a fintech ad. Low-key tension during problem section, warmer resolve during product demo.
- **Pacing:** The script runs ~200 words for 90 seconds. Aim for 2.2 words/second average. Let pauses breathe.

## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Total runtime:** ~88–92 seconds. Tighten VO pacing in demo section if running long.
- **Captions:** Include closed captions for accessibility. Many HOA board members are 60+.
- **Screen recording resolution:** 1440p minimum. Record at 60fps, export at 30fps for smooth playback.
- **Product screens:** Use a demo community ("Maplewood Heights HOA") with realistic fake data — $480K budget, 127 units, real-looking proposals.
- **End card:** Static frame holds for 3 seconds after final VO line. Include clickable link overlay for digital distribution.
- **Music fadeout:** Should resolve on the CTA section, not cut abruptly.
- **Thumbnail frame:** Capture the "Every vote. Every dollar. Every document." text card at 0:38 — strong thumbnail candidate.
