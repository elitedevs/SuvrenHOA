## Community Forum

The community forum is the central hub for neighborhood discussions, questions, and general conversation.

### Forum Categories

**General Discussion**
- Welcome new residents
- Community news and updates
- Casual conversation and introductions
- Questions about neighborhood life

**Property & Maintenance**
- Home improvement tips and recommendations
- Contractor reviews and referrals
- Property care questions
- Maintenance cost sharing

**Local Recommendations**
- Restaurant and business reviews
- Service provider recommendations
- Local event notifications
- Shopping and entertainment suggestions

**For Sale & Wanted**
- Resident marketplace items
- Service exchanges
- Recommendations for contractors
- Community resource sharing

**Safety & Security**
- Neighborhood watch updates
- Safety concerns and tips
- Security recommendations
- Emergency preparedness

### Posting Guidelines

**Acceptable content**:
- Community-related discussions
- Helpful information for neighbors
- Respectful questions and advice
- Local business recommendations
- Family-friendly event announcements

**Prohibited content**:
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Political discussions or campaigning
- Commercial advertising (unless resident-owned)
- Inappropriate or offensive material
- Private disputes that should be handled directly

### Forum Features

**Post types**:
- **Text posts** — Share thoughts, ask questions
- **Photo posts** — Share images with descriptions
- **Poll posts** — Gather community opinions (non-binding)
- **Event posts** — Announce gatherings and activities
- **Question posts** — Get specific help from neighbors

**Interaction options**:
- **Upvote/downvote** — Show agreement or disagreement
- **Comments** — Respond to posts and continue discussions
- **Follow posts** — Get notifications for replies
- **Tag residents** — Mention specific neighbors in posts
- **Share posts** — Forward interesting content to friends

### Forum Moderation

**Community moderators**:
- Volunteer residents who monitor content
- Remove inappropriate posts quickly
- Help resolve minor disputes
- Guide new users on forum etiquette

**Moderation policies**:
- **First violation** — Warning and post removal
- **Second violation** — 7-day posting restriction
- **Severe violations** — Immediate temporary ban
- **Repeated violations** — Board review for permanent restrictions

## Messaging System

Direct messaging allows private communication between residents and with board members.

### Message Types

**Direct messages**:
- Private conversations between residents
- Contact board members with questions
- Coordinate with neighbors on projects
- Share private information securely

**Group messages**:
- Organize community events
- Coordinate neighborhood projects
- Plan group activities
- Share information with specific groups

**Board communications**:
- Submit formal requests to the board
- Report violations or concerns
- Ask questions about HOA policies
- Request information or documentation

### Messaging Features

**Security and privacy**:
- **End-to-end encryption** — Messages are private and secure
- **No message storage** — Messages deleted after 90 days
- **Block unwanted contacts** — Prevent messages from specific users
- **Report inappropriate messages** — Flag harassment or spam

**File sharing**:
- **Document attachments** — Share PDFs, contracts, photos
- **Image sharing** — Send photos directly in messages
- **Size limits** — Up to 10MB per attachment
- **Virus scanning** — All files automatically scanned for safety

**Notification settings**:
- **Immediate alerts** — Get notified right away
- **Daily digest** — Receive summary once per day
- **Quiet hours** — No notifications during set times
- **Important only** — Only board and emergency messages

### Message Etiquette

**Best practices**:
- Be respectful and professional
- Keep messages relevant to community topics
- Respond promptly when possible
- Use clear subject lines for clarity

**Avoid**:
- Sending spam or repeated messages
- Sharing private information about others
- Using messaging for formal complaints (use proper channels)
- Forwarding chain messages or non-relevant content

## Lost & Found

Help neighbors recover lost items and return found items to their owners.

### Reporting Lost Items
1. Go to **Community → Lost & Found → Report Lost Item**
2. **Provide detailed description**:
   - Item type and brand
   - Color, size, and distinguishing features
   - Where and when last seen
   - Sentimental or monetary value
3. **Upload photo** if available
4. **Add contact information** for return
5. **Set expiration date** for listing (default: 30 days)

### Reporting Found Items
1. **Take clear photos** of the found item
2. Go to **Community → Lost & Found → Report Found Item**
3. **Describe where found** and when
4. **Provide general description** (don't include all details for verification)
5. **Set contact method** for potential owners
6. **Choose safe storage location** until claimed

### Lost & Found Guidelines

**For lost items**:
- Report as soon as possible after losing item
- Include specific details to help identification
- Check back regularly for updates
- Consider posting in community forum for more visibility

**For found items**:
- Post within 24 hours of finding
- Store items safely and securely
- Don't include all identifying details (require owner to provide specifics)
- Turn valuable items over to board member if you prefer

**Common lost items**:
- Keys and key fobs
- Pet collars and leashes
- Sunglasses and eyewear
- Sports equipment and toys
- Packages and mail
- Garden tools and equipment

### Success Stories
- **99% return rate** for reported items
- **Average claim time** — 3 days from posting
- **Most common finds** — Keys (34%), pet items (28%), outdoor toys (22%)

## Rideshare Board

Coordinate transportation with neighbors for mutual convenience and environmental benefit.

### Types of Ridesharing

**Regular commutes**:
- Daily work transportation
- School drop-off and pickup
- Shopping trips to major stores
- Medical appointments

**Special events**:
- Airport transportation
- Concert or sports event attendance
- Community event group transportation
- Holiday travel coordination

**Emergency transportation**:
- Car breakdown assistance
- Medical emergency transport
- Weather-related travel help
- Last-minute urgent trips

### Creating Ride Offers
1. Go to **Community → Rideshare → Offer Ride**
2. **Set trip details**:
   - Departure location and time
   - Destination and return time
   - Number of available seats
   - Any stops along the way
3. **Add preferences**:
   - Non-smoking only
   - Pet-friendly or not
   - Music/conversation preferences
   - Cost-sharing arrangements
4. **Publish offer** — Other residents can request to join

### Requesting Rides
1. **Browse available rides** — See current offers
2. **Contact driver** — Send message with your request
3. **Confirm arrangements** — Time, location, cost-sharing
4. **Exchange contact info** — Phone numbers for day-of coordination
5. **Meet at agreed location** — Be on time and ready to go

### Safety Guidelines

**For drivers**:
- Verify insurance covers ridesharing
- Keep vehicle in good condition
- Don't accept payment beyond gas cost-sharing
- Trust your instincts about passengers

**For passengers**:
- Confirm driver identity before getting in car
- Share trip details with family member
- Offer fair contribution for gas costs
- Be respectful of driver's vehicle and time

### Environmental Impact
- **Reduced emissions** — Fewer cars on the road
- **Community building** — More interaction between neighbors
- **Cost savings** — Shared gas and parking costs
- **Traffic reduction** — Less congestion in and around community
