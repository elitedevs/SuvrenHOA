## What is SuvrenHOA?

SuvrenHOA is a modern homeowner association platform that uses blockchain technology to manage our community transparently and efficiently. Think of it as a digital tool that handles:

- **Property records** — Your home ownership is represented by a secure digital certificate (NFT)
- **Voting & governance** — Participate in community decisions with transparent, tamper-proof voting
- **Dues payments** — Pay your HOA dues using USDC (a stable digital currency)
- **Community features** — Connect with neighbors, book amenities, and stay informed
- **Maintenance** — Submit requests and track progress
- **Documents** — Access HOA documents, bylaws, and meeting minutes

Everything is recorded on the blockchain, which means all transactions, votes, and decisions are permanent and verifiable by anyone.

## What You'll Need

Before you start, make sure you have:

1. **A computer or smartphone** with internet access
2. **A digital wallet** (we recommend MetaMask for beginners)
3. **Your property information** (address, lot number)
4. **Some USDC** for dues payments (the board can help you get started)
5. **An email address** for notifications

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Visit the Platform
1. Go to [suvrenhoa.faircroft.com](https://suvrenhoa.faircroft.com)
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Provide Basic Information
1. Enter your **full name** (as it appears on property records)
2. Provide your **email address**
3. Enter your **property address** exactly as recorded
4. Add your **phone number** (optional but recommended)
5. Click **"Continue"**

### Step 3: Verify Your Identity
The system will ask you to verify you're a legitimate property owner:
1. Upload a **photo of your driver's license** or ID
2. Upload a **recent utility bill** showing your address
3. The board will review and approve within 24-48 hours
4. You'll receive an email when approved

## Connecting Your Wallet

A digital wallet holds your property NFT and USDC for payments. If you don't have one:

### Installing MetaMask (Recommended)
1. Go to [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)
2. Click **"Download"** and install the browser extension
3. Follow the setup wizard to create a new wallet
4. **IMPORTANT**: Write down your recovery phrase and store it safely
5. Add some ETH (around $20) for transaction fees

### Connecting to SuvrenHOA
1. Log into your SuvrenHOA account
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top right
3. Select **"MetaMask"** from the list
4. Approve the connection in your wallet
5. Your wallet address will appear in your profile

### Getting USDC
You'll need USDC to pay dues:
1. Buy USDC on exchanges like Coinbase or Kraken
2. Send it to your MetaMask wallet address
3. The board can help you with this process if needed

## First Login Walkthrough

Once your account is approved and wallet connected:

### Dashboard Overview
Your main dashboard shows:
- **Property summary** — Your lot details and status
- **Outstanding dues** — Any payments needed
- **Recent announcements** — Community updates
- **Active proposals** — Items you can vote on
- **Upcoming events** — Community calendar
- **Maintenance requests** — Any open tickets

### Navigation Menu
The left sidebar includes:
- **Dashboard** — Main overview page
- **My Property** — Detailed property information
- **Governance** — Proposals and voting
- **Treasury** — Financial overview and dues
- **Community** — Forum, directory, events
- **Maintenance** — Request and track services
- **Documents** — Access HOA records
- **Profile** — Account settings

### Quick Actions
Look for these helpful buttons:
- **Pay Dues** — Make payments quickly
- **New Maintenance Request** — Report issues
- **View Proposals** — See what's up for vote
- **Book Amenity** — Reserve community spaces

## Understanding the Dashboard

### Property Status Card
Shows your current property information:
- **Address and lot number**
- **Property NFT ID** — Your unique digital certificate
- **Ownership status** — Verified owner, tenant, etc.
- **Dues status** — Current, overdue, or upcoming

### Notifications Panel
Important updates appear here:
- **Dues reminders** — Payment deadlines
- **Proposal alerts** — New items to vote on
- **Maintenance updates** — Work order progress
- **Community announcements** — HOA news

### Quick Stats
At-a-glance community information:
- **Active proposals** — Items currently up for vote
- **Upcoming events** — Community calendar
- **Recent decisions** — Latest voting results
- **Treasury balance** — Current community funds
