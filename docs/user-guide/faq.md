## Getting Started

### What is SuvrenHOA?
SuvrenHOA is a blockchain-based homeowner association platform that uses modern technology to manage our community transparently and efficiently. Your home ownership is represented by a secure digital certificate (NFT), and you can pay dues using USDC (a stable digital currency). All transactions, votes, and decisions are recorded permanently on the blockchain for complete transparency.

### Do I need to be tech-savvy to use SuvrenHOA?
Not at all! The platform is designed for everyday homeowners. While it uses advanced blockchain technology behind the scenes, the interface is user-friendly. We provide orientation sessions for new residents and ongoing support to help everyone feel comfortable using the system.

### How do I create my account?
1. Visit the SuvrenHOA website
2. Click "Create Account" and provide your basic information
3. Upload a photo ID and utility bill to verify your identity
4. The board will review and approve your account within 24-48 hours
5. Once approved, you'll receive an email with login instructions

### What if I don't have a digital wallet?
We'll help you set up a digital wallet during your account creation process. We recommend MetaMask for beginners, and our support team can guide you through the installation and setup. You can also attend our monthly "Digital Wallet 101" workshops.

### Can I still pay dues if I don't want to use cryptocurrency?
Yes! While USDC payments are preferred and have lower fees, we also accept ACH bank transfers and credit/debit card payments. However, these traditional methods have higher processing fees that are passed on to the resident.

### What happens to my account if I sell my property?
When you sell your property, your account is transferred to the new owner along with the property NFT. You'll need to provide the new owner's wallet address, and there's a 48-hour cooling period before the transfer completes. Your account will be closed once the transfer is complete.

## Property & NFTs

### What is a property NFT?
An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique digital certificate that represents your home ownership. Think of it as a digital deed that can't be forged, lost, or altered. It contains all your property information, ownership history, and HOA compliance status.

### What if I lose access to my NFT?
SuvrenHOA has multiple safety measures:
- **Backup wallet registration** — You can register a backup wallet when you create your account
- **Emergency contacts** — Trusted family members can help with recovery
- **Board recovery process** — As a last resort, the board can help with legitimate recovery cases
- **48-hour cooling period** — Prevents accidental transfers

### Can someone steal my property NFT?
The blockchain technology makes theft extremely difficult, but you need to protect your wallet:
- Keep your seed phrase (recovery words) written on paper, never digitally
- Use strong, unique passwords for your wallet
- Never share your private keys with anyone
- Register a backup wallet with the HOA
If someone gains access to your wallet, they could potentially transfer your NFT, which is why security is so important.

### How do I transfer my property to a family member?
1. Ensure all dues and fees are current
2. Have the family member create their SuvrenHOA account
3. Go to "My Property" → "Transfer Property"
4. Enter their wallet address and confirm the transfer
5. Wait for the 48-hour cooling period to complete
6. Family transfers may require additional documentation (marriage certificate, etc.)

### What information is stored in my property NFT?
- Legal property description (lot number, boundaries, square footage)
- Home details (year built, bedrooms, bathrooms, features)
- Ownership history (complete chain of ownership)
- HOA compliance status (dues, violations, standing)
- Property tax information
- Architectural improvements and approvals

### Can I edit my property information?
Some information can be updated by homeowners (contact info, rental status, pets, vehicles), while other information (square footage, lot size) requires official verification and board approval. Structural changes need proper architectural review and permits.

## Payments & Dues

### How much are the monthly dues?
Monthly dues are currently $285 per property, or $3,270 annually (with a 5% discount for annual payment). Dues cover operating expenses like landscaping, utilities, insurance, maintenance, and security, plus contributions to the reserve fund.

### What forms of payment do you accept?
- **USDC** (preferred) — Instant processing, lowest fees
- **ACH bank transfer** — 3-5 business days, $2.50 fee
- **Credit/debit card** — Instant, but 3% convenience fee

### What is USDC and how do I get it?
USDC is a "stablecoin" — a cryptocurrency that's always worth exactly $1.00. You can buy it on exchanges like Coinbase, Kraken, or Binance, then transfer it to your wallet. The board can help you with this process if you're new to cryptocurrency.

### What happens if I'm late paying dues?
- **1-10 days late** — No penalty (grace period)
- **11+ days late** — $25 late fee added
- **30+ days late** — Additional $25 fee each month
- **90+ days late** — Loss of voting rights until current
- **Severely delinquent** — Collection and lien procedures may begin

### Can I set up automatic dues payments?
Yes! You can set up automatic payments using:
- **USDC auto-pay** — Automatic monthly USDC transfers from your wallet
- **ACH auto-pay** — Automatic bank transfers (includes $2.50 fee each month)
Both methods require maintaining adequate balances in your account.

### What if I have a dispute about my bill?
1. Contact the treasurer through the platform messaging system
2. Provide specific details about the disputed charges
3. Submit any supporting documentation
4. The treasurer will review and respond within 5 business days
5. If unresolved, you can appeal to the full board

## Governance & Voting

### How do I vote on community proposals?
1. Go to "Governance" → "Active Proposals"
2. Click on the proposal to read full details
3. Select your choice (Yes, No, or Abstain)
4. Confirm your vote in your connected wallet
5. Pay the small transaction fee (usually under $1)
Your vote is recorded permanently on the blockchain and cannot be changed.

### Who can vote?
Property owners have one vote per property they own. Tenants cannot vote, but landlords can vote for their rental properties. All voters must be in good standing (dues current, no major violations).

### What types of proposals require different approval levels?
- **Routine operations** — 25% quorum, 50%+1 approval
- **Financial decisions** — 35% quorum, 60% approval
- **Governance changes** — 50% quorum, 65% approval
- **Constitutional changes** — 67% quorum, 75% approval

### Can I delegate my vote to someone else?
Yes! You can delegate your voting power to another property owner. Go to "Governance" → "Vote Delegation" to set up delegation. You can revoke this at any time. Note that board members cannot receive delegated votes to avoid conflicts of interest.

### What if I disagree with a board decision?
You have several options:
- Attend board meetings to voice concerns during public comment
- Submit proposals to change policies you disagree with
- Run for the board in annual elections
- Request formal board reconsideration of specific decisions

### How often are board elections held?
Board elections are held annually in April. Positions have staggered terms:
- President, Treasurer, Member-at-Large: Even years
- Vice President, Secretary, Member-at-Large: Odd years
The Member-at-Large position is elected every year to provide fresh perspective.

## Community Features

### How do I access the community forum?
Once you're logged into the platform, go to "Community" → "Forum". You can post questions, share information, and connect with neighbors. Please follow community guidelines and keep discussions respectful and relevant.

### How do I reserve amenities like the clubhouse or pool?
1. Go to "Amenities" → "Reservations"
2. Select the amenity and desired date/time
3. Provide event details and expected attendance
4. Pay the rental fee in USDC
5. Submit your reservation request
Most reservations are approved automatically, but large events may require board approval.

### Can I post items for sale in the community marketplace?
Yes! Go to "Community" → "Marketplace" to list items for sale or services you offer. Only residents can post, and we ask that you keep prices fair and descriptions honest. Commercial businesses are not allowed to advertise.

### How do I report a lost item or found item?
Use the "Community" → "Lost & Found" feature. For lost items, provide detailed descriptions. For found items, provide general descriptions (require the owner to provide specific details for verification). Store found items safely until claimed.

### What community events are regularly scheduled?
- **Monthly community potluck** — First Friday of each month
- **Board meetings** — Second Tuesday, open to all residents
- **Kids' movie night** — Third Saturday at clubhouse
- **Senior coffee hour** — Every Wednesday morning
- **Seasonal events** — Holiday parties, community cleanup days, summer BBQs

### How can I volunteer or get involved in community activities?
Check the "Community" → "Volunteer Opportunities" section, attend board meetings, join committees, or contact board members directly. We always need volunteers for events, committees, and community improvement projects.

## Maintenance & Services

### How do I submit a maintenance request?
1. Go to "Maintenance" → "Submit Request"
2. Choose the priority level (Emergency, Urgent, Standard, Routine)
3. Describe the issue in detail and upload photos
4. Submit the request and receive a tracking number
5. Monitor progress through the platform

### What's considered an emergency maintenance request?
Emergencies require immediate attention and include:
- Safety hazards (broken glass, exposed electrical wires)
- Water leaks affecting multiple properties
- Security system failures
- Any situation posing immediate danger to residents
For true emergencies, also call our 24/7 emergency hotline.

### Do I need approval for home improvements?
Yes, for exterior modifications visible from the street or common areas:
- Paint color changes
- Roof replacements
- Deck or patio additions
- Fence installation
- Major landscaping changes
Interior modifications don't require approval.

### How long does architectural review take?
- **Simple requests** — 2 weeks
- **Complex projects** — 30 days
- **Appeals** — Additional 30 days
Submit requests early in your planning process to avoid delays.

### What contractors are approved to work in the community?
We maintain a list of pre-approved contractors who are licensed, bonded, and insured. While you can use other contractors, approved ones have negotiated rates and proven track records. Check "Maintenance" → "Approved Contractors" for the current list.

### Who is responsible for maintenance of what?
- **HOA responsibility** — Common areas, landscaping, exterior building maintenance, amenities
- **Resident responsibility** — Interior of homes, individual lot landscaping, driveways, personal property
- **Shared responsibility** — Some items like certain utilities or shared walls may be shared based on specific circumstances

## Security & Safety

### How do I report a safety or security incident?
For emergencies, call 911 first. For non-emergencies, use "Safety" → "Report Incident" on the platform. Provide detailed information including date, time, location, and description of what happened.

### What security measures are in place?
- **24/7 professional monitoring** — Security service watches camera feeds
- **Gated access** — Controlled entry with key fobs and guest codes
- **Surveillance cameras** — 24 cameras covering common areas and entry points
- **Emergency call boxes** — 6 locations with direct connection to security
- **Evening patrols** — Security vehicle patrols 6 PM - 6 AM

### How do I get a guest access code?
Go to "Community" → "Visitor Management" and create a visitor registration. You can generate temporary access codes for guests valid from 1 day to 1 week. Codes are free for your first 5 guests per month.

### What are the community quiet hours?
- **Weekdays** — 10 PM to 7 AM
- **Weekends** — 11 PM to 8 AM
- **Holidays** — 11 PM to 9 AM
- **Pool area** — Closes at 9 PM every day

### How do I file a noise complaint?
Try talking to your neighbor first. If that doesn't resolve the issue, go to "Safety" → "Noise Complaint" and document the noise including dates, times, duration, and impact. The board will investigate and take appropriate action.

### What should I do if I lose my wallet or phone with my access credentials?
Contact the board immediately to temporarily disable your access codes. Once you have a replacement device, contact support to restore your access. This is why it's important to have backup wallet registration and emergency contacts on file.

## Technical Support

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder. If you still have trouble, contact support.

### My wallet isn't connecting to the platform. What should I do?
1. Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
2. Ensure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked
3. Try refreshing the page and reconnecting
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. If still having issues, contact technical support

### I can't find my property NFT in my wallet. Where is it?
Check that you're looking in the correct wallet address. If you recently transferred or restored your wallet, the NFT should still be there. Contact support if you believe your NFT is missing — we can help verify its location on the blockchain.

### The platform is running slowly. Is there a problem?
Platform performance can be affected by:
- High blockchain network traffic (higher fees, slower processing)
- Your internet connection
- Browser issues (try clearing cache or using different browser)
- Scheduled maintenance
Check our status page for any known issues, or contact support if problems persist.

### How do I update my contact information?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "Profile" → "Personal Information"
3. Update your contact details
4. Save changes
Some changes (like property ownership information) may require board verification.

### I'm having trouble with USDC payments. What can I do?
Common issues and solutions:
- **Insufficient USDC** — Make sure you have enough USDC plus a small amount of ETH for transaction fees
- **Network congestion** — Transaction fees may be higher during busy periods
- **Wrong network** — Make sure you're using the Ethereum network
- **Wallet not connected** — Ensure your wallet is connected to the platform
Contact support if you're still having trouble with payments.

### Who do I contact for different types of problems?
- **Technical issues** — Platform technical support
- **Account problems** — Board secretary or platform administrator
- **Payment issues** — Board treasurer
- **Community concerns** — Board president or appropriate committee chair
- **Emergencies** — 911 first, then emergency hotline
- **General questions** — Any board member or use the community forum

### Is there a mobile app?
Currently, SuvrenHOA works through web browsers on mobile devices. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future development. The website is mobile-friendly and works well on phones and tablets.

### How do I stay updated on platform changes and new features?
- **Community announcements** — Posted on the platform
- **Email newsletters** — Monthly updates sent to all residents
- **Board meeting minutes** — Technology updates discussed in meetings
- **Community forum** — Platform updates and discussions
- **Annual meetings** — Major technology changes presented annually
