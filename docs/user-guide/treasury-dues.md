## How Dues Work

HOA dues fund community operations, maintenance, and improvements. SuvrenHOA uses transparent blockchain-based payments for accountability.

### Current Dues Structure
**Monthly dues**: $285 per property
**Annual payment option**: $3,270 (5% discount = $150 savings)
**Due date**: 1st of each month
**Grace period**: 10 days (no late fee until 11th)
**Late fee**: $25 per month after grace period

### What Dues Cover
**Operating expenses** (70% of dues):
- Landscaping and grounds maintenance
- Common area utilities (lighting, irrigation)
- Insurance (liability, property, D&O)
- Management and administrative costs
- Regular maintenance and repairs
- Security services
- Amenity maintenance (pool, playground, clubhouse)

**Reserve funds** (30% of dues):
- Major capital improvements
- Emergency repairs
- Equipment replacement
- Future community enhancements
- Legal and professional services
- Technology infrastructure

### Dues Calculation
Dues are calculated based on:
- **Property size** — All properties pay same base amount
- **Amenity access** — Pool, clubhouse, playground included
- **Annual budget** — Approved by community vote each year
- **Reserve requirements** — State-mandated minimum reserves
- **Operating costs** — Actual expenses from previous year

### Annual Dues Review
Each December:
1. **Board prepares budget** for following year
2. **Community review period** — 30 days for questions and feedback  
3. **Budget proposal vote** — Requires 35% quorum, 60% approval
4. **Dues adjustment** — Limited to inflation + 5% without special vote
5. **Implementation** — New rates effective January 1st

### Setting Up Auto-Pay

**USDC auto-pay** (recommended):
1. Go to **"Treasury"** → **"Payment Settings"**
2. Connect wallet with sufficient USDC balance
3. **Authorize recurring payments** for dues amount
4. **Set payment date** (1st-5th of each month)
5. **Configure notifications** for payment confirmations

**ACH auto-pay**:
1. **Link bank account** through secure Plaid integration
2. **Verify micro-deposits** (1-2 business days)
3. **Set recurring payment** schedule
4. **Maintain adequate balance** — Overdraft fees are your responsibility

## USDC Payments

**USDC (USD Coin)** is a stable cryptocurrency pegged to the US dollar, providing the benefits of blockchain payments without price volatility.

### What is USDC?
- **Stablecoin** — Always worth exactly $1.00
- **Backed by reserves** — Fully collateralized by US dollars and Treasury bills
- **Regulated** — Issued by regulated financial institutions
- **Transparent** — Reserve backing audited monthly
- **Widely accepted** — Supported by major exchanges and platforms

### Getting USDC

**Option 1: Buy on exchanges**
- **Coinbase, Kraken, Binance** — Major exchanges support USDC
- **Direct purchase** with bank transfer or debit card
- **Transfer to your wallet** after purchase
- **Fees**: Usually 0.5-1% for purchases

**Option 2: Convert from other crypto**
- **Use decentralized exchanges** like Uniswap or SushiSwap
- **Convert ETH, BTC, or other tokens** to USDC
- **May have price slippage** on large conversions
- **Gas fees apply** for transactions

**Option 3: Receive as payment**
- **Get paid in USDC** by clients or employers
- **Direct deposit** to your wallet address
- **No conversion fees** if already in USDC

### USDC Payment Benefits
**For residents**:
- **Instant settlement** — Payments clear immediately
- **Lower fees** — No credit card or ACH processing fees
- **Transparency** — All payments visible on blockchain
- **Global access** — Pay from anywhere in the world
- **No chargebacks** — Eliminates payment disputes

**For the HOA**:
- **Reduced processing costs** — Lower fees than traditional payments
- **Instant availability** — No waiting for funds to clear
- **Automatic accounting** — All transactions recorded on blockchain
- **Reduced disputes** — Immutable payment records

### USDC Security
- **Use reputable wallets** — MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, hardware wallets
- **Verify transaction details** — Always check recipient address
- **Keep private keys safe** — Never share with anyone
- **Use multi-signature** for large amounts
- **Regular backups** — Store recovery phrases securely

**Pre-approved categories**:
- Emergency repairs (up to $500 before approval)

- Office supplies for HOA business

**Requires pre-approval**:

### Reimbursement Process
1. **Submit request** through Treasury → Reimbursements
2. **Upload receipts** — Clear photos or PDFs required
3. **Provide detailed description** of expense and HOA benefit
4. **Select expense category** from approved list
5. **Add any supporting documentation**
6. **Submit for review**

### Approval Timeline
- **Small requests** (<$100): 24-48 hours
- **Medium requests** ($100-$500): 3-5 business days
- **Large requests** (>$500): Next board meeting
- **Emergency repairs**: Same-day approval for critical issues

### Payment Methods
**USDC payment** (preferred):
- Immediate payment upon approval
- No processing fees
- Direct to your wallet address

**ACH bank transfer**:
- 3-5 business day processing
- Requires verified bank account
- $2 processing fee deducted from reimbursement

**Check payment**:
- Available for residents without digital wallets
- 7-10 business days for delivery
- No additional fees

### Documentation Requirements
All reimbursement requests must include:
- **Original receipts** — Clear, complete documentation
- **Business justification** — How expense benefits the HOA

**Capital improvements**:
- Major infrastructure projects
- Amenity additions or upgrades
- Technology implementations
- Emergency equipment replacement

**Emergency situations**:
- Natural disaster recovery
- Major insurance claim deductibles
- Legal settlement payments
- Critical safety repairs

**Regulatory compliance**:
- Government-mandated improvements
- Code compliance upgrades
- Environmental remediation
- Accessibility improvements

### Assessment Approval Process
1. **Board proposal** — Detailed project scope and cost analysis
2. **Community notification** — 30 days notice before vote
3. **Information sessions** — Q&A meetings for residents
4. **Community vote** — Requires 50% quorum, 65% approval
5. **Payment schedule** — Board sets collection timeline

### Assessment Payment Options
**Lump sum payment**:

**Payment plan**:
- **Up to 24 months** for assessments over $1,000
- **Equal monthly installments** added to regular dues
- **No interest** if payments made on time
- **Default acceleration** — Full balance due if payments missed
